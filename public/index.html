At Infinity Spine Center, Scottsdale Chiropractor Dr. Thoma is committed to
providing NUCCA Chiropractic Care and auto accident care. NUCCA Chiropractic
is a gentle, specific, and scientific based chiropractic treatment for
misalignment of the upper cervical spine. Dr. Thoma is the only NUCCA Chiropractic
member practicing in Scottsdale.
In addition to chiropractic, therapies include acupuncture, dry needling, corrective
movement therapy, red and near infrared light therapy, nutrition, and lifestyle
plans that restore and cultivate optimal health and wellness for all ages
and cases including acute, maintenance, and injuries as a result of an auto accident.
We use NUCCA Chiropractic supplemented with corrective movement
therapy designed for your specific posture and muscle and joint dysfunction.
